ABOUT US

Who Are We?

Our Advantages in Custom Parts Manufacturing

Glasses.3D Co., Ltd is a professional online CNC parts manufacturing company in Serbia. Founded in 2022. Our plant area has already exceeded 500 m².
With the capabilities of CNC Machining, CNC turning, CNC milling, SWISS CNC precision machining, Die casting, Finishing and assembly etc(Services). Glasses.3D provides one-stop service from parts design, instant quote to rapid prototyping.
Glasses.3D have 10 CNC machines, which are all the most advanced equipment.We provide turn-key services from prototypes to assembled products.
